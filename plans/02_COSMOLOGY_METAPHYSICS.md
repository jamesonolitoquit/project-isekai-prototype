# 02 — Cosmology & Metaphysics

> Project Isekai — Luxfier Alpha
> Master Reference: `plans/00_MASTER_REFERENCE.md`
> Dependencies: `01_META_AUTHORITY.md`

This layer answers how existence functions. Constrains magic, death, time, corruption, and progression.
If this is fuzzy, balance breaks and AI rulings become arbitrary.

---

## 2.1 Structure of Existence

### 2.1.1 The Chaos Realm
- Primordial substrate in which worlds exist
- Not evil, not ordered, not sentient
- Operates on probabilistic instability
- Produces: dimensional drift, conceptual decay, cross-world artifacts
- **Chaos does not create meaning. It erodes it.**
- Failure mode to avoid: treating Chaos as a villain or god

### 2.1.2 Luxfier (Anchored World)
- A stabilized reality pocket within Chaos
- Stability maintained by Lux-Ar
- Laws of reality are locally consistent but globally fragile
- **Luxfier is not natural — it is maintained**

### 2.1.3 Lux-Ar (World Anchor)
- Crystal World Tree / metaphysical stabilizer
- Functions:
  - Anchors time flow
  - Maintains soul recycling integrity
  - Dampens Chaos interference
- Key constraints:
  - Can be damaged
  - Cannot be fully destroyed without collapsing Luxfier
  - Damage propagates subtly over centuries
- **Lux-Ar is infrastructure, not divinity**

---

## 2.2 Time

### 2.2.1 Time Flow Model
- Linear-forward at macro scale
- Locally elastic at micro scale
- Properties:
  - Cannot be reversed without cost
  - Cannot be duplicated
  - Cannot be erased cleanly
- **No timelines. No branching multiverses inside Luxfier.**

### 2.2.2 Temporal Debt
- Incurred by: rewinds, time magic, fate manipulation
- Manifestations:
  - Increased failure probability
  - Narrative friction
  - Chaos bleed
  - Temporal anomalies
- **Debt never disappears — only transforms**

---

## 2.3 Souls

### 2.3.1 Soul Definition
A soul is:
- A persistent identity waveform
- Bound to causality
- Finite in integrity
- **Souls are not immortal by default**

### 2.3.2 Soul Wavelengths
- Every soul has a unique resonance
- Determines: relic compatibility, magic affinity ceilings, corruption resistance
- **Soul wavelength cannot be changed, only distorted**

### 2.3.3 Soul Integrity
- Measured implicitly, not numerically
- Damaged by: Sin weapons, corruption, repeated resurrection, temporal debt
- Low integrity results in: memory loss, personality drift, permanent death risks

---

## 2.4 Death

### 2.4.1 Death Is Absolute (With Cost Exceptions)
- Normal death ends the current existence
- Soul proceeds to recycling or dissolution
- Resurrection: rare, always costly, never perfect
- **No casual revive loops**

### 2.4.2 Soul Recycling
- Lux-Ar processes souls
- Outcomes: reincarnation (memory-stripped), dissolution into Chaos, fragment persistence (ghosts, echoes)
- **Reincarnation is not guaranteed**

### 2.4.3 Undeath
- Occurs when soul recycling is interrupted
- Always unstable, always decaying
- **Undeath is a metaphysical error state**

---

## 2.5 Magic

### 2.5.1 Magic Source
- Magic is: controlled Chaos interference, filtered through Lux-Ar stabilization, expressed via intent + structure
- **Magic does not violate reality; it stresses it**

### 2.5.2 Magic Costs
Every spell incurs at least one:
- Physical cost
- Mental cost
- Soul cost
- Environmental cost
- **High-tier magic must incur permanent cost**

### 2.5.3 Magic Ceilings
Limits imposed by: soul wavelength, body tolerance, world resistance
- **Power scaling is asymptotic, not exponential**

---

## 2.6 Corruption

### 2.6.1 Corruption Definition
- Accumulated Chaos imprint
- Alters probability, desire, and perception
- **Corruption is not mind control**

### 2.6.2 Corruption Progression
Stages:
1. Subtle bias
2. Compulsion
3. Identity erosion
4. Reality distortion
5. Collapse or transformation

**Corruption offers power before punishment.**

### 2.6.3 Cleansing
- Possible but incomplete
- Always leaves scars
- Some corruption is permanent

---

## 2.7 Fate & Free Will

### 2.7.1 Fate
- Statistical tendency, not prophecy
- Stronger around relics and anchors
- Can be bent, not broken

### 2.7.2 Free Will
- Exists within constraints
- Choices always have consequences
- **No destiny exemption for protagonists**

---

## 2.8 Dimensional Boundaries

### 2.8.1 Other Worlds
- Exist in Chaos Realm
- Rarely intersect Luxfier
- Artifacts may cross unintentionally

### 2.8.2 Human World
- Not myth
- Not connected by stable gate
- Human arrivals are anomalies
- Haruto's arrival is historically significant because it was **statistically impossible**, not foretold

---

## 2.9 Meta Constraints (Gameplay-Relevant)

- No infinite loops
- No perfect information
- No free resurrection
- No consequence-free rewinds
- No absolute immunity

**These are cosmological truths, not balance hacks.**

- Explains why gods aren't omnipotent
- Justifies relic rarity and danger
- Makes corruption tempting
- Makes death meaningful
