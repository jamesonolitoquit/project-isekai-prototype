# Milestone 5: The living Board & Ancestral Echoes

This milestone focuses on transitioning the world from a static backdrop into an interactive, erosive board game environment while deepening the player's biological and cognitive identity.

## Phase 45: Tactical World Interaction (The Grid)
- **Isometric Board**: 3D interactive tile-grid replacing static map textures.
- **Spatial Verbs**: `SEARCH`, `HARVEST`, `INTERACT` logic in `spatialInteractionEngine.ts`.
- **World Erosion**: Player actions (Fire, Scars) leave persistent visual/mechanical markers.
- **Persistent Scars**: High-impact actions evolve biomes in future Epochs.

## Phase 46: Diegetic Workstations & Materiality
- **Station-Locked Crafting**: Alchemy Labs, Anvils, and Terminals as physical board assets.
- **Experimentation UI**: Ingredient drag-and-drop with "Blind Fusing" logic.
- **Wealth Pouch**: 3D money asset on the tabletop with era-appropriate scaling (Gold, Credits, Scrip).

## Phase 47: Ancestral Tapestry (Race/Sub-race)
- **Passive Skill Trees**: Fixed biological nodes unique to each Race (Elven, Dwarven, Human, etc.).
- **Material Passives**: Innate potential modifiers (e.g., Elven +15% Arcane XP).
- **Synergy Modifiers**: Race-Codec alignment (e.g., Human + Industrial = AP Bonus).

## Phase 48: Chronos-Study (Time-Skips)
- **Batch Tick Engine**: Ability to "Meditate" or "Study" for X days in 1 click.
- **World Advancement**: Global entropy and faction plots continue during study skips.
- **Interruption Risk**: Ambushes or Weather Events can abort high-tier spell learning.

## Phase 49: Mastery Marketplace & Paradox Learning
- **Mentorship**: Recruitable NPCs that boost study speed via Reputation/Gold.
- **Chrono-Sanctum**: Instant learning at the cost of **Paradox Debt**.
- **Glitch feedback**: High Paradox Debt causes visual board destabilization.

## Phase 50: Cognitive Modifiers (INT vs WIS)
- **Processing (INT)**: High Intelligence boosts XP gain rate and Daily caps.
- **Retention (WIS)**: High Wisdom reduces Skill Decay and boosts Discovery rates.
- **Affinity Mapping**: Skills tied to specific mental anchors (e.g., Alchemy -> INT).

## Phase 51: Stat Compendium & Character Creation
- **Diegetic Glossary**: Hoverable definitions of all 6 stats in creation/profile.
- **Era-Specific Flavor**: STR is "Torque" in Cyberpunk, "Might" in Medieval.
- **Formula Transparency**: Clear UI indicators of how stats affect combat results.
