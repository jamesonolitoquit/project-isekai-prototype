# DSS 12 — Production & Material Processing

## 12.1 The Production Contract
Production is not an instantaneous menu toggle. It is a **Physical Causal Event** requiring three components:
- **The Protocol/Blueprint:** Domain-specific knowledge (INT-gated).
- **The Materials:** Physics requirement (Conservation of Matter).
- **The Station:** Precision requirement (Environmental stability).

## 12.2 Workstation Complexity
Every workstation (DSS 01) has a **Complexity Capacity**.
- **Improvised (Tier 1):** Max Protocol Level: 10. Multiplies Failure Chance by 2.0x.
- **Master (Tier 3):** Max Protocol Level: 75. Adds +2 to the Dice Altar success range.
- **Advanced/Causal (Tier 5):** Can process unstable materials (Template-dependent) without immediate explosion.

## 12.3 Matter Transformation & Volatility
Transformation is subject to **Material Volatility**.
- **The Processing Ratio:** Small deviations in `DEX` or `WIS` checks during the "Processing" phase result in **Impurity**.
- **Impurity Effects:**
  - **Minor:** Reduced efficacy of the output.
  - **Major:** Toxicity or Instability (temporary attribute penalty).
  - **Catastrophic:** Workstation detonation (Physical damage + Infection/Hazard risk).

## 12.4 Structural Shaping & Thermal Cycles
- **Environmental Management:** Maintaining the correct processing environment requires a `STR` (Physical) or `INT` (Mental) check per 10 Ticks.
- **Finishing:** The final `DEX` check determines the `Durability` floor. A failed check creates a "Brittle" item that breaks at 2x speed.

## 12.5 Inscription & Enchantment
- **Mana Draw:** Enchanting requires drawing from the Tile's `Mana Saturation`.
- **The Misfire Loop:** If the `INT` requirement for an enchantment is > than the Player's current level, every Tick has a 5% chance to trigger a **Paradox Leak**, attracting "Shadow" entities.
