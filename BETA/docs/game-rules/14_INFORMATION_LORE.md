# DSS 14 — Information, Reputation & Knowledge Scarcity

## 14.1 The Information Lag (Physical News)
In a world governed by the **Atomic Tick**, information is not instant. It is a **Physical Asset** that travels.
- **Propagation Speed:** News of an `EventMagnitude > X` travels at a rate of 1 Tile per 600 Ticks (15 minutes).
- **Reputation Silos:** Your social weight (CHA-based) is different in every FactionID until the "Information Wave" synchronizes them.
- **Outdated Data:** Maps and NPC locations in the "Sepia Tone" Fog of War (DSS 10) may be false if the player hasn't visited the Tile within the current **Epoch**.

## 14.2 Knowledge as a Finite Commodity
Skills and Spells are not magically known. They are **Stored Data**.
- **The Scroll/Data-Core Rule:** To learn a protocol (DSS 12), you must physically possess the storage medium.
- **INT-Gated Compression:** High Intelligence allows a player to "Remember" more protocols simultaneously. If INT is low, learning a new high-tier protocol may force the "Forgetting" of an old one (Soft Cap logic).
- **Knowledge Destruction:** Libraries, records, and data-cores can be physically destroyed (Phase 4: Resolution), potentially removing a specific protocol from the **World Template** entirely.

## 14.3 Secret Hierarchies & Discovery
- **The Whisper Mechanic:** High `PER` (Perception) and `WIS` (Wisdom) allow players to intercept "Information Rumbles" from neighboring Tilies.
- **Lore Discovery:** Unique world lore is not flavor; it provides a **Resolution Bonus** (+2) to specific Faction or Divine interactions if the player "knows the secret."
