# GAME RULES: TIER 2B — PARADOX DEBT & TEMPORAL LAW

## I. THE CONCEPT (The Price of Choice)
Time is not a "Simulation Reset." It is a persistent ledger. Every attempt to reverse the flow of the **Atomic Tick** (Save-scumming, Rewinding) creates a **Paradox Branch**.

## II. THE INVARIANT (The Formal Paradox Formula)
For every **Branch Event (B)**, the world's **ParadoxDebt (P)** increases:

```
P_new = P_old + (EventMagnitude * InformationGained / TemporalDivergence)
```

Where:
*   **EventMagnitude**: Scale of the bypassed event (Combat: 20, Death: 50, Faction Fall: 100).
*   **InformationGained**: New knowledge the Player (Actor) now possesses (Enemy Stats, Loot Location).
*   **TemporalDivergence**: Total Ticks since the Branch Point.

## III. PARADOX EFFECTS (The Decay Cascade)
Paradox is a tracking value (0-100) that exerts pressure on **Tier 0** reality.

### Threshold 1: Bleed (30%)
*   **Visual**: RGB Sepia tint on the 3D Stage.
*   **Systemic**: +1 Difficulty to all Sanity checks.
*   **Narrative**: "Glitched" dialogue from NPC observers.

### Threshold 2: Bleach (70%)
*   **Visual**: Grayscale desaturation. Shadows stretch unnaturally.
*   **Systemic**: 15% chance for "Anomaly Spawns" (Hostile Void Huskys) during any Tick.
*   **Divine**: Scrutiny triggered. Divine Intervention costs increase by 200%.

### Threshold 3: Reality Fault (100%)
*   **Systemic**: The **Dice Altar** becomes "Faulty." A d20 roll may display a success, but the engine resolves it as a failure. 
*   **Outcome**: The Vessel is forcibly "Reset" to the nearest **Sanctified Zone** (World Hub). The Ledger remains; the time is spent.

## IV. CLEANSING (The Restoration Protocol)
Paradox does not decay passively. It must be processed.
*   **Study/Meditation**: Requires **Continuous Ticks (1,000+)** in a Library or Shrine.
*   **Sacrifice**: Consuming high-tier Artifacts at a Forge to "pay" the temporal debt.

---

## V. SYSTEM IMPACT SCOPE: GLOBAL
Paradox affects all Actors (Player and NPC). If a Faction uses Chronos-Study to win a war, they too accumulate Paradox Debt.
