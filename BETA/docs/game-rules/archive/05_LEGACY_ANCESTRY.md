# GAME RULES: LEGACY & ANCESTRAL POWERS

## I. ANCESTRAL TAPESTRY (Phase 47)
The player is a "Vessel" for a line of Echoes.

### 1. The Tree Nodes (Passive Perks)
Unlocking nodes in the **Tapestry (Right Wing Overlay)** requires:
*   **XP Cost**: Earned through Study/Combat.
*   **Race Lock**: Nodes are tied to `currentRace` (Human, Elf, Void, Cyber).
*   **Alignment Check**: High Paradox can lock "Sanctified" nodes.

### 2. Node Tiers
*   **Tier 1 (Base - 100 XP)**: +1 Str/Int/Agi.
*   **Tier 2 (Niche - 500 XP)**: Resistance to specific Hazard (e.g., Ash Storm).
*   **Tier 3 (Master - 1000 XP)**: Unique Active Ability (e.g., "Chronos Pulse").

---

## II. SOUL ECHOES (The Legacy System)
When a character reaches "Ascension" or dies, their features are archived.

### 1. Ascension Rewards
*   **Legacy XP**: Players gain 10% of their base XP as "Legacy Coins" for the next vessel.
*   **Relic Binding**: 1 Unique Item from the previous life can be marked as "Equipped" (Bound) by default.

### 2. Generational Paradox
*   **Persistence**: Paradox (0-100) does **not** reset on character death.
*   **Scaling Difficulty**: Every generation increases "Paradox Bleed" base levels by +1. 
*   **The Wipe**: Only a `Full Epoch Reset` (M48) cleanses generational debt.

---

## III. RELICS & ARTIFACTS (Phase 15)
Unique items are sentient (Artifact Sentience Loop).

### 1. Relic Rebellion
*   **Check (Every 1000 Ticks)**: If `Relic_Stance < 20`, it may refuse an action.
*   **Effect**: Random failure of Attribute Checks for 100 Ticks.

### 2. Runic Infusion
*   Items can be upgraded at **Workstations (Forge)**.
*   **1 Slot**: +3 to primary Stat.
*   **3 Slots (Full)**: +10% Experience Gain.
