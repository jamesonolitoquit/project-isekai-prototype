# GAME RULES: TIER 2C — THE TRIAD OF VITALS (BIOLOGICAL CONSTRAINTS)

## I. THE CONCEPT (The Pressure of Form)
A **Vessel** is not an immortal avatar. It is a biological or bio-mechanical interface with reality. Every **Atomic Tick** (Tier 1) places a tax on its integrity.

## II. THE STATS (The Vital Metrics)
Survival is a **Threshold-Based DECAY** engine.

### 1. VIGOR (Physical Constitution)
What the vessel is physically capable of.
*   **Decay rate**: -1% per Hour (Standard Mode). 
*   **Action Cost**: Moving 1 Tile = -5% Vigor. 1 Combat Turn (Pulse) = -2% Vigor.
*   **Threshold 1 (90%+): Primed**
    *   **Effect**: +1 Physical (STR, AGI, END) rolls.
    *   **Visual**: Golden glow on the left Dossier.
*   **Threshold 2 (<30%): Fatigued**
    *   **Effect**: -1 to ALL dice rolls. 15% chance for Action Interrupt (failure).
*   **Threshold 3 (0%): Collapse**
    *   **Effect**: Movement is disabled. Sanity begins to drop at 5% per tick.

### 2. NOURISHMENT (Chemical Energy)
The reservoir of potential power.
*   **Decay rate**: -5% only when "Significant Action" (Combat/Travel) occurs.
*   **Depletion**: At 0%, the **Starvation** debuff is applied.
    *   Natural HP Regeneration is 100% disabled.
    *   HP Decays by -5 per 100 Ticks.
*   **Saturation (Bonus)**: High-quality meals (Phase 46) provide a "Saturation Timer" (100+ Ticks where Nourishment does not decay).

### 3. SANITY (Neural Stability)
The cognitive bridge between Player and Vessel.
*   **Decay rate**: Passively lost in **Cursed** or **Paradox-Bleed** biomes.
*   **Threshold 1 (<25%): Static**
    *   Visual "Static" on the Stage. 
    *   NPC Dialogue options become randomized or "Corrupted."
*   **Threshold 2 (0%): Fugue State**
    *   The **AI Director** takes control of the vessel for 100 Ticks (Tier 1).
    *   System Scope: Regional.

---

## III. RECOVERY (The Rest Protocol)
Vitals are restored through **Phase 1 (Chronos-Batch)** Ticks.
*   **Short Rest (1 Hour)**: +10% Vigor.
*   **Long Rest (8 Hours)**: +100% Vigor (at Inn/Safe Zone), +50% Vigor (in Wilds).

## IV. SYSTEM IMPACT SCOPE: LOCAL (Vessel Only)
While Factions (Tier 3) have their own logic, this specific engine only applies to the player's active Vessel.
