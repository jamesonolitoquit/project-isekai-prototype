# DSS 13 — Domain Interventions & Causal Backlash

## 13.1 Intervention as Causal Alteration
High-tier actions (Magic/Tech/Ritual) are not "fire-and-forget" events. They are **Direct Alterations of Reality** that generate **Paradox Debt** (DSS 03).
- **The Activation Cycle:**
  1. **Draw:** Pulling energy from the Tile's `Resource Saturation`.
  2. **Channel:** Maintaining focus (INT/CON check).
  3. **Release:** Directing the effect (WIS/DEX check).

## 13.2 Intervention Complexity & Tiers
Interventions are rated by their `Magnitude`.
- **Tier 1 (Surface):** No Paradox Debt. Small utilities.
- **Tier 3 (Domain):** Adds +1 Paradox Debt per use. Mid-level system impacts.
- **Tier 5 (Causal):** Adds +10 Paradox Debt per use. High-level timeline/world alterations.

## 13.3 Protocol Failures & Backlash
Every high-tier action has a **Misfire Range** on the Dice Altar.
- **Backlash Roll (Natural 1-3):**
  - **Burn:** Immediate decrease in resource pool or temporary attribute penalty (f(INT)).
  - **Sanity/System Drift:** Decrease in cognitive stability or system integrity (f(WIS)).
  - **Reality Bleed:** Triggers a localized **Temporal Divergence** event, summoning an "Echo" or "Anomaly" from a failed timeline/dimension.

## 13.4 High-Magnitude Rituals
Multiple players or NPCs can combine their `FaithMass` or `ActionBudget` for **World-Shifting Rituals**.
- **The Ritual Circle:** Requires specific static world markers (Shrines/Spires).
- **The Synchronicity Rule:** All actors must commit to the same **Resolution Stack (Phase 4)**. If one actor fails their `WIS` check, the entire ritual backlashes.
- **Victory Rituals:** Used to trigger an **Era Shift** or the **Mirror Path** victory condition.

## 13.5 Causal Feedback Loops
Casting multiple Tier 3+ spells in the same Tile within 600 Ticks (15 mins) creates a **Causal Static** effect:
- All further spells in that area have a +5 modifier to their **Misfire Range**.
- The `Mana Saturation` for that Tile is depleted until the next **Batch Compression** cycle.
